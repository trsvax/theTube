# Workflow Conventions

## After pushing changes

When the user says they'll "read it" or "look at it" after a push, include clickable URLs to the live site. Format: `[post-slug](https://thetube.today/posts/<slug>)` for posts. Use the slug (filename without `.md`).

After pushing, keep it minimal: "Done. [slug](https://thetube.today/posts/<slug>)"
